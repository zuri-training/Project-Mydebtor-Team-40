Who We Are

StuDebt is formed by a team of diligent, diciplined & hardworking individuals from across the country who identified a common problem & put their heads together to find a solution.
Debt being a common & reccuring issue in many schools has been a hard knot to crack. Schools are faced with a financial dilemma when parents refuse to make payment for the fees of their wards before transferring them to another school without trace.
StuDebt has come up with a way to help schools handle this tough situation.

Our Purpose

We are here to act as a body or platform where schools can run to whenever they need to list a debtor or find a debtor.
As an authorized school admin on the platform, you have the permission to make a list of debtors in your institution so that other school admins can find this information & make good admission choices into their own institutions.
